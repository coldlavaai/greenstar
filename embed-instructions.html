This guide shows you how to embed the VAPI chat widget on any website. The widget is completely self-contained and works on any hosting environment.

Step 1: Basic Integration (Simplest Method)

Add this single line before your closing </body> tag:

That's it! The widget will appear in the bottom-right corner of your website.
