Home battery storage

Solar battery storage enhances the efficiency and value of a solar power system by storing excess energy for use during peak times or at night. This reduces grid reliance, offers protection during power cuts, and cuts electricity costs by allowing stored energy use instead of peak rate grid power. You can also charge your batteries on low cost overnight tariffs to maximise your systems returns. Homes with solar batteries may also see increased property value due to their energy efficiency and improved epc rating. Overall, a solar battery boosts energy independence, cost savings, and sustainability, making it a valuable addition to a home solar system.

Batter storage for your home

  • 1. Get the maximum power from your panels

    If you don’t have a battery, and your panels don’t produce enough energy for your needs, your system draws on the grid, and you pay for this electricity. Batteries give you much more control over your energy because they reduce your vulnerability to peak time electricity prices. They can also power your home during a power cut.



  • 2. You can store cheap energy from the grid

    Although it may be difficult to believe during the current energy crisis when rates are so high, energy prices fall overnight to as low as 7 pence per kwh, and energy suppliers are paid to take energy off the grid. Use the app to charge your batteries with cheap overnight electricity for use during peak rate times. 

  • 3. Reduce your carbon footprint

    Solar batteries bring your property closer to energy independence and self-sufficiency. Solar energy creates far less pollution than fossil fuels and reduces your home’s carbon footprint for years. They also improve your homes EPC rating and its value


  • 4. Slash your electricity bill

    One of the most significant benefits of battery storage is that they slash your electricity costs as you avoid the fees associated with utility companies. Some people turn to solar batteries to disconnect from the grid entirely

  • 5. Virtually no maintenance.

    At this point, it’s also worth mentioning that batteries require very little maintenance once installed. They are also as quiet as a mouse.

Get the Most From Your Battery

All of our inverter and battery options utilise a real-time interactive app, an essential tool for monitoring and managing your energy usage and storage. With these apps, you can set grid charges to allow your batteries to recharge at lower-cost, off-peak rates offered by top energy providers. Overnight rates can be as low as 7.5 pence per kWh, approximately 75% cheaper than peak electricity prices. We’ll thoroughly assess your energy usage and peak load to recommend the optimal battery storage solution for your needs. We offer high end battery storage solutions from leading manufactures such as Solar Edge, Fox ESS, and Eco Flow.
